Purpose

The Sunspotters site is an informational site for photographers looking to nature photography around these particular locations. It will be a guide to photographic locations in the area as well as help them plan for the specific weather conditions they might want to photograph.

Domain Name

The domain name "sunspotters.net" is available and would be the domain for the site.
