Target Audience

Photographers wanting to visit Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven to take nature photography. The site will give current weather conditions, extended forecast and when sunrise and sunset are expected. They want additional details on the terain they might encounter to get the location and gear they need to carry. This type of photographer wants to know the lighting and weather conditions so they can plan ahead and then photograph under specific conditions. There will also be a Gallery page for the photographers to share their photos.


Visitors & Scenarios

Sandi Johnson

Demographics:

45 year old mother of 4. She is currently a stay-at-home mom.

Purpose of Visit:

Nature photography is hobby and a way for Sandi to get some exercise. She visits the site so she can find out the details she needs to and learn more about photography when she takes her photos.


Marc Thompson

Demographics:

28 year old photography enthusiast. High school Biology teacher. Masters in Biological science.

Purpose of Visit:

Marc has goals to build a photography career. He's working on building his portfolio. He has particular types of weather he would like to photograph. He visits the site so he knows exactly the right time of day and days when the conditions will hopefully be present to for his photography. He enjoys exploring nature. He also uses his photography to bring the outdoors to the high school classes he teaches.


Tomeca Howard

Demographics

65 year old grandmother of 2 and mother of 4.

Purpose of Visit:

She enjoys hiking on a regular basis and likes to know the conditions of various trails before she goes. She enjoys seeing the beautiful photography taken by others posted in the gallery.
