Fredericksburg Local Small Business Group

Who Are We?

We are an active group of local small business working together to help Fredericksburg, Virginia have a thriving active community. We love our city and want it to be a place where people live, work and enjoy being together. We support each other when things are difficult through various crossover events that draw customers into Downtown Fredericksburg and bring traffic to each of our businesses. We believe we all grow be helping each other to grow.
